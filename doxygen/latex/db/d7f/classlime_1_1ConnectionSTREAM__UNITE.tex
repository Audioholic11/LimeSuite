Bulk S\+PI write/read transaction. 

The transact\+S\+PI function is capable of bulk writes and bulk reads of S\+PI registers in an arbitrary IC (up to 32-\/bits per transaction).

The read\+Data parameter may be N\+U\+LL to indicate a write-\/only operation, the underlying implementation may be able to optimize out the readback.
